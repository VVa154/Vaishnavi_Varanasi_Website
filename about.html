My Story

From a young age, I have always been driven by curiosity. I did not just memorize facts, I questioned why things worked the way they did. This curiosity naturally drew me toward Math and Physics and shaped the way I approach learning and problem-solving.

This passion led me to pursue an MBA in Data Science and Analytics. During this time, Statistics became my thing. I loved uncovering patterns, building models, and using numbers to tell meaningful stories. It was also when I discovered a love for coding, solving complex problems, and finding practical ways to create business value.
